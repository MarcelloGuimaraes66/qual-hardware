## Risk review

- Runtime/model supply chain: official candidates are pinned separately. The telemetry probe has reproducible packaged candidate hashes, provenance and SBOM evidence, but commercial use remains blocked by the empty approval map, unresolved legal/SBOM reviews for candidates, missing approved runtime/model artifacts, and physical gates.
- Scientific continuity: partial commercial repetitions are never combined across interruption; qualification restarts at repetition 1.
- Disk pressure: admission and mid-phase checks stop generation before reserve loss and clean only owned files.
- Cancellation ordering: worker events are serialized, late progress/results are ignored after cancellation, and subprocesses killed by Stop are classified as cancelled rather than hardware failures; both packaged regressions now pass.
- Import abuse: byte ceilings, bounded streaming decompression, strict schemas, canonical digests, Ed25519 signatures, device trust, and append-only conflict records.
- Recommendation overstatement: exact compatibility gates, lowest compatible exact result, three-distinct-configuration extrapolation, and benchmark-only planning labels.
- Rollback: kernel, resume, exchange, and evidence policy can be disabled independently without deleting records.
