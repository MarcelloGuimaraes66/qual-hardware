# Research

- Apple official specifications confirm M4/M4 Pro media engines and unified memory, and M4 Max/M3 Ultra Mac Studio configurations.
- Apple store pages provide dated whole-system price references; allocations inside an integrated system are explicitly estimates, not separately purchasable component quotes.
- ASUS official S5606 and GU605 specifications confirm the Core Ultra and mobile NVIDIA configurations.
- The Aiquimist-supplied ASUS S5606CA configuration is operationally observed, but its camera capacity is not benchmark-validated.
