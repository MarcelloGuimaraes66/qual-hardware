# Implementation verification

- Exact ASUS mode returns the requested template for all policies and never silently substitutes another computer.
- Conservative minimum-policy estimates at 1080p H.264/15 FPS CPU decode and remote GPT: about 19 cameras for one frame/10 s, 13 cameras for 1 FPS video and 1 camera for high-resolution 10 FPS video.
- These are estimates tied to a 35% sustained mobile factor and explicit FFmpeg limit; matching telemetry must replace them for validation.
- macOS tests prove Apple-only selection, three distinct designs and rejection of incompatible local-AiQ/GPU-decode demand.
- Regression suite covers desktop-only entrypoint and loopback binding.
