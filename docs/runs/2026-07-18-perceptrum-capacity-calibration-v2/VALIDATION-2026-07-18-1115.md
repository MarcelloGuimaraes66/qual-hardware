## Cross-platform evidence

Shared contracts and tests cover win32, darwin and linux. Perceptrum CI uses
Windows 2025, macOS 26 arm64 and Ubuntu 24.04, Node 24, native packaging and
desktop/runtime smokes. Windows and Ubuntu are compile-ready by design, but
physical Windows 11 and Ubuntu GNOME/Wayland homologation remains required
before claiming full runtime validation on those two operating systems.

## Remaining evidence gates

High-confidence extrapolation cannot be manufactured from one physical anchor.
Import two or three additional compatible computer calibrations and a curated,
signed public-evidence snapshot. Until then, unsupported targets correctly stay
`reference_only` rather than receiving a purchase-grade capacity claim.
